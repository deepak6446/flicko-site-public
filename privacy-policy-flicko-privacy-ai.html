Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 Nov 2024

Introduction

Welcome to FlickO Private AI ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application ("the App").

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

  • User-Provided Information: We do not require you to provide any personal information to use the App.
  • Automatically Collected Information: The App does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Usage Data

  • Offline Usage: All AI interactions occur locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or upload any data from your conversations with the AI models to our servers.
  • Model Downloads: When you choose to download AI models, the App connects to external servers (e.g., Hugging Face) to retrieve the models. During this process, standard network data may be transmitted, such as your IP address, to the third-party server.

How We Use Your Information

  • Providing Services: To enable the download of AI models and ensure the App functions correctly.
  • No Data Upload: We do not upload any user data or personal information to our servers.
  • Improving the App: Since we do not collect user data, we do not use your information for analytics or improvements that require data collection.

Third-Party Services

1. Advertising

  • Ad Networks: The App displays advertisements to generate revenue. Third-party ad networks may collect certain information to provide personalized ads.
  • Information Collected by Ad Networks: This may include device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings or visiting the ad network's privacy policy.

2. Model Downloads

  • External Sources: AI models are downloaded from third-party sources like Hugging Face. These services may collect standard network data during downloads.
  • Privacy Policies: Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these third-party services for more information.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App. Your continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
